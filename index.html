BIO

Career

Starting in 2012 this talented Breda based producer became an artist on the rise under the name Di-veck. Di Veck's sound consists of a big-room sound and a cutting edge drop to make club dance-floors shake on their grounds. Earlier in 2012 Dj Di-veck signed his publishing deal with Cloud9, giving him the opportunity to expand his sound all over the world.

Collabs

With a buzzer of a track, ending on the #1 spot in the EDM Buzz Chart in the final week before it's release, Di-veck entered the EDM Scene with "Party Hard" released on Hardwell's label Revealed Recordings begin oktober 2012. The Track was premiered during Hardwell's Tomorrowland show 2012 and got major attention in the dance scene. With early support from fellow Revealed Recordings artist Dannic & JordyDazz and later on Quintino, David Guetta, Benny Benassi, Sander van Doorn, Swanky Tunes and others, the path is cleared for Di-veck to become a major in the scene. 2013, starting off with a remix for Daleri on Trice Recordings as a request from Armada Music, is set to be the year to more popularity on an already fast growing fan-base. With a 2nd release on Revealed Recordings presents Miami 2013 called "Bouncy Betty" and a an upcomming release on WoohaRecords, Di-veck is ready to leave his mark in the world of EDM.
